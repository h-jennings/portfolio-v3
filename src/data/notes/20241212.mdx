Spent the past week diving into [Lexical](https://lexical.dev/) and the world of
text editor frameworks. Rich text editing is one of those problems that looks
simple on the surface but gets deeply complex once you start pulling at the
edges.

After a lot of reading and tinkering with open-source code, I built a
feature-complete replacement for our legacy `<RichTextarea />` component
(previously based on the now-deprecated `react-rte`). It was one of those
projects where the learning curve was steep, but the result was worth it.
