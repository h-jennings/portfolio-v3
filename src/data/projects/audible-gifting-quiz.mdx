Audible wanted to turn gift-giving into something that felt personal and fun. The challenge was building an experience that felt playful and immersive while still being easy for the marketing team to update on their own.

The result is a CMS-driven quiz built with Contentful, React Three Fiber, GSAP, and Blender models. Everything from the questions to the 3D assets is manageable through the CMS, so it stays fresh year-round without needing a developer in the loop.

Credit to Steve Krichten for the 3D work and animations.
