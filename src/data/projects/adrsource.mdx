ADRSource is a small firm of experienced mediators, arbitrators, and special
magistrates based in Florida. They needed a site that felt credible and
established while still being easy for the team to manage day-to-day.

The site is fully CMS-driven through Hygraph, with GraphQL codegen for type-safe
data fetching. Team member profiles, resource posts, and page content all live
in the CMS, so the firm can update their roster and publish articles on their
own.

The frontend layers in staggered entrance animations, an auto-scrolling team
carousel, and scroll-linked color blur transitions to give the site a sense of
motion without overwhelming the content.
