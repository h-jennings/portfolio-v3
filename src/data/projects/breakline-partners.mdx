BreakLine needed a dedicated site for its employer partners in the defense and
dual-use sectors, something tailored to their priorities: hiring velocity,
clearance-ready talent, and time-to-productivity. It went from idea to shipped
in two weeks.

The page guides visitors through a conversion-focused narrative — an animated
hero, value-prop pillars backed by hiring stats, a social proof logo grid, and a
partner inquiry form — each section building momentum toward the call to action.
Scroll-triggered theme transitions shift the page from light to dark as visitors
move deeper. The logo grid uses vertically scrolling columns with hover-speed
effects, and highlight regions respond to a Motion-powered custom cursor.

Built with React Router v7 in framework mode, Sanity CMS with visual editing,
and a custom inquiry form backed by an intake service layer.
